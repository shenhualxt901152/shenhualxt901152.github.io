PasteKeyboardX Support

PasteKeyboardX is a custom keyboard for saving short clips and inserting them from the keyboard.

Common setup checks

  • Add PasteKeyboardX in iOS Settings > General > Keyboard > Keyboards.
  • Full Access lets the keyboard load saved clips from the shared App Group container.
  • iCloud Sync requires an iCloud account and stores synced clips in the user's iCloud Private Database.
  • Clipboard import happens only after tapping the clipboard button in the keyboard.

Contact

For support, contact the developer through the App Store support contact listed for PasteKeyboardX.
