PasteKeyboardX Privacy Policy

PasteKeyboardX stores short text clips that you create so you can insert them from the custom keyboard.

Data handled by the app

  • Clips and tags that you create in the app.
  • Clipboard text only after you tap the clipboard button in the keyboard.
  • iCloud Sync data when you turn on iCloud Sync.

Clipboard

PasteKeyboardX does not continuously monitor the clipboard. Clipboard content is read only after you tap the clipboard button in the keyboard, and the text is used to create a clip.

iCloud Sync

When iCloud Sync is enabled, clips and tags are stored in your iCloud Private Database so they can sync across your devices. PasteKeyboardX does not use a developer-operated server for clip sync.

Sharing and tracking

We do not sell user data. We do not use clips, clipboard text, or iCloud Sync data for advertising tracking. We do not share clip content with third-party analytics or advertising services.

Deletion

You can delete clips in the app. Deleted clips are removed from local storage and, when iCloud Sync is enabled, deletion records are synced to your iCloud Private Database.
